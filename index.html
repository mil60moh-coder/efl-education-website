About this website

This is a free educational website created for learners and teachers of English.

The website is designed and developed by an Inspector General of English who worked for many years within the Ministry of Education and has extensive experience in curriculum design, classroom teaching, assessment, and inspection.

Purpose

  • Provide structured, progressive, and reliable EFL content across educational cycles
  • Focus on meaningful language use and learner autonomy
  • Ensure alignment between teaching, practice, and assessment

Principles

  • Free and open to all learners and teachers
  • Non-commercial and ad-free
  • Designed to evolve progressively through reflection and refinement
