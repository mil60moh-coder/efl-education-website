Unit 1 – Lesson 1

Let’s have fun

This lesson introduces vocabulary and structures related to free time activities and games.

Official syllabus – Lesson objectives

  • Identify and talk about free time activities.
  • Express likes and preferences using simple structures.
  • Use basic linkers to give reasons.

Core vocabulary

  • fishing
  • museum
  • go camping
  • riding a bike
  • gardening

Grammar points

  • Like / enjoy + verb + -ing
  • Linker: because

21st-century skills and values

  • Communication and interaction
  • Making choices and expressing preferences
  • Responsible use of free time

Core Principle

Work smarter, not harder.
Think carefully, plan, and make intelligent choices rather than just repeating work.
