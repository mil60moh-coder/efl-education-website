# EFL Education Website

A **free, modular English as a Foreign Language (EFL) learning website** covering **Primary, Preparatory, and Secondary cycles**. Created by an **Inspector General of English** with years of experience in the Ministry of Education.  

This website is designed to be fully **customizable, update-friendly, and modular**, allowing progressive additions, edits, or deletions of content.

---

## Features

- Covers **all three cycles**:
  - **Primary**: Years 4–6  
  - **Preparatory**: Years 7–9  
  - **Secondary**: Years 1–4  
- Includes **official syllabus**:
  - Learning objectives  
  - Core vocabulary lists  
  - Grammar points  
  - 21st-century skills and values  
- **Lessons**: structured with vocabulary, grammar, reading, writing, speaking, and activities  
- **Exercises and tests** for self-assessment  
- **Self-check pages** for independent practice  
- **Core Principle reminder** on every page:  
  > **Work smarter, not harder. Think carefully, plan, and make intelligent choices rather than just repeating work.**  
- Fully **modular folder structure** for easy updates and maintenance  
- **Responsive navigation** with header and footer for all pages  
- **Customizable design** with preferred colors (orange, gold, blue-green, grey) and fonts (Fira/Roboto/Segoe for headings, Georgia/Cambria for body text)
