## About this website

This is a free educational website created for learners and teachers of English.

The website is designed and developed by an Inspector General of English who
worked for many years within the Ministry of Education and has extensive
experience in curriculum design, classroom teaching, assessment, and inspection.

It is conceived as a professional educational resource that reflects institutional
standards, pedagogical coherence, and long-term classroom experience.

---

## Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide structured, progressive, and reliable
EFL content across different educational cycles and levels, with a clear focus on:

- meaningful language use  
- learner autonomy and responsibility  
- transparency of learning objectives  
- alignment between teaching, practice, and assessment  

---

## Principles

This website is:

- free and open to all learners and teachers  
- non-commercial and ad-free  
- independent of private publishing interests  
- designed to evolve progressively through reflection and refinement  

Its content is developed with the same rigour expected in formal educational
contexts, while remaining accessible and learner-friendly.

---

## Use and development

The website is continuously reviewed and improved.  
Content may be adjusted, expanded, or refined in order to better serve learners’
needs and maintain pedagogical coherence.

Feedback and professional dialogue are welcome.
